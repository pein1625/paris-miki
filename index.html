About Paris Miki

In 1930, Paris Miki founding President Yoshio Tane started Seikakudo Tokeiten in Himeji, Japan, a retail store specializing in the sales and repairs of clocks, precious metals, and eyewear. Mr. Tane is devoted to providing outstanding customer care and teaches all his employees to "Always listen to our customer's voice and treat all people with kindness and respect." Following his philosophy, Paris Miki expanded from a single retailer store to become one of the world’s largest optical chain stores with over 1,000 locations throughout the United States, Europe, Australia and Asia.
